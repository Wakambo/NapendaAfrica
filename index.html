About Us - Our Passion for Africa

Napenda Africa Safaris was established and incorporated in the tourism industry in 2008. Founded with a deep love for East Africa, our mission is to share the beauty and wonder of this region with you.

Our team of experienced guides and staff have been in the hospitality industry for many years, dedicated to ensuring your trip is enjoyable, safe, and truly memorable.

We are a small and intimate company that prides itself on offering personalised safari experiences led by enthusiastic experts.

We also believe in sustainable and responsible tourism, working closely with local communities and conservation organisations to protect the fragile environment we call home.
